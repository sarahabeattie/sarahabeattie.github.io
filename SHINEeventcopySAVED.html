Shine! | Thursday, Oct. 5 | 6-9PM

Shine is an evening for the San Francisco community to come together and celebrate the young women of Oasis, to elevate awareness of issues impacting their lives, and to raise critical funds to help sustain Oasis’ programs. The gala is attended by a sold-out crowd of 200+ people every year, and is generously hosted at Twitter HQ. Join us for a lively night of great food and wine, an extensive silent auction featuring the best of San Francisco, and community!


Investing in girls pays significant dividends for our communities for years into the future, empowering girls to stay engaged academically and economically, fostering healthy families, communities, and policy in their wake. Proceeds from Shine go directly to programs, and in particular:

  • Enhance civic engagement curriculum across the programmatic array in response to evolving social realities.
  • Support the expansion of GROW, our new workforce development program, in partnership with UCSF.
  • Connect the next generation of women leaders with the business minds of today to build a better San Francisco together.

Sponsorships

Our sponsors represent leaders in the fields of technology, communications, finance, automotive services, philanthropy, and creative industries with a strong presence here in San Francisco. In addition to their financial contribution, sponsors invest in our city’s future by supporting Oasis girls long after Shine through job site visits, career mentorship, subject-based workshops, and more.

Silent Auction

The silent auction offerings at Shine boast the best experiences, food & wine, art, and wares of our local San Francisco community. Come and indulge in the things you’ve always wanted and a few you didn’t know you wanted!


Past items have included:

  • Vineyard tours, tastings, and a stay at a private villa in Sonoma
  • Private training sessions
  • Gift certificates for custom jewelry
  • Private workshops at local chocolateries or wineries
  • Sailing adventures in the Bay


If you have a suggestion for a good silent auction item, or would like to donate an item, please contact Ly Nguyen or Ayesha Kirpalani.
